Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 July 2026

Mind Reset Daily Uploader is a private creator tool used by the owner of the TikTok account Mind Reset Daily to publish approved short-form videos.

Information processed

The app may process basic TikTok account information required for authorization, access tokens required for publishing, generated video files, captions, hashtags, privacy settings, AI-generated content labels and upload status.

How the information is used

The information is used only to operate the publishing workflow and to publish videos approved by the account owner to the owner’s social media accounts.

No third-party publishing

The app is not a public upload service. No third-party users are allowed to upload or publish content through this app.

Sharing

The app does not sell personal data. The app does not share personal data with advertisers. Information is shared with platform providers only as required to authorize and publish approved content.

Token storage and revocation

Access tokens are stored only for operating the publishing workflow. Access can be revoked by the user through TikTok account settings or by contacting the app owner.
